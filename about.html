About




Blerdy Womanist was founded by Beatrice Opara. An MA Gender Studies Graduate, who is a black nerd and womanist.

Blerdy Womanist is a space to increase the visibility of women of colour in the technology industry

  • Her mission is to connect Black nerds who simultaneously identifies as a womanist.
  • Empowering women of colour in the technology industry
  • Increasing visibility of women in colour in the tech industry
  • Educate women of colour the different roles available in tech
  • Inspiring the future generation to consider technology.
